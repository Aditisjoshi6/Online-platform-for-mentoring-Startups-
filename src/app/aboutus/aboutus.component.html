About Us

A startup is a young company that is just beginning to develop. Startups are usually small at initial stage and mainly handled by students or youths. These companies offer a product or services that is not currently being offered elsewhere in the market. This public domain dose not only provide the elliptical information but also mentor with the various procedures to be looked upon. Online platform for mentoring startups gives guidance for the needy and enhances the culture of innovation and technology. This platform provides mentor who will objectively assess the state of your startup and the direction in which it is headed.

The main object is to provide the basic guidance or mentorship like:

  • Assistance regarding the legalization of the company
  • Business planning
  • Product marketing support and strategy
  • Building contacts
